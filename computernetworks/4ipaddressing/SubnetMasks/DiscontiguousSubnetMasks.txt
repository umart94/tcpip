cisco devices do not support discontiguous subnet masks.

11110000.11111111.00000110.11000000 = 240.255.3.191

this example does not have continous 1's or continous 0's


only continuous subnet masks are supported.

11111111.11110000.00000000.00000000 = 255.240.0.0
11111111.111111111.11000000.00000000 = 255.255.192.0
