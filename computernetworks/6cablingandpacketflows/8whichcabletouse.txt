straight through cables are used when we connect pC to a switch , hub , bridge.


two pcs connection is made by crossover cable.

in this case we have two mdi devices.
example 10baset or 100baset.


Straight through cable
used when
router to switch
pc to switch
pc to bridge
pc to hub


crossover cable
router to router
pc to pc
hub to hub
switch to switch
bridge to switch
pc to server
hub to switch
router to router

Auto MDI / MDIX

MDI devices are routers or PCS

MDIX are medium dependent interface crossover devices (switches hubs)

auto mdi / mdix allows for automatic switching once a cable is connected.
auto detect cables types cross over / straight through
no need to worry about cable types.

a straight cable can be used between hubs for example.
